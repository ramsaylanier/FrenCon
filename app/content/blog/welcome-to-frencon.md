# Welcome to FrenCon 2026!

We're excited to have you here. FrenCon is a board game convention organized by and for friends.

## What to Expect

- **Game Nominations** — Nominate games you'd like to play
- **Roundtable Discussions** — Share ideas and topics
- **TTRPG Polling** — Vote on tabletop RPGs to run
- **Videos** — Watch recordings from past events
- **Merch** — Suggest merchandise ideas

Sign in to participate and make FrenCon 2026 the best one yet!
